Who We Are

A Community Open to Everyone

IVC — the International Volunteers Community — is a youth Catholic community in Seoul, under the Archdiocese of Seoul. We are a family of 100+ young people from different cultures, backgrounds, and nationalities who come together to build friendships and serve in the name of Jesus Christ.

"Even if you're not Catholic — don't worry. You are welcome to our family."

Our motto, Duc in Altum — "Put out into the deep" (Luke 5:4) — calls us beyond comfort to serve wholeheartedly. Under the spiritual guidance of Father Fabiano and Team Leader Michael, IVC is a place to grow, connect, and support each other as a community that truly feels like family.

Our Foundation

Three Pillars of IVC

Everything we do flows from three core commitments — rooted in the Gospel, shared in community, offered in service.

01

Faith

Nourished by prayer, Scripture, and the Sacraments, we are anchored in the love of Jesus Christ. Our faith is the wellspring of all we do — and what brings us together every week.

02

Friendship

Youth from across the world — different cultures, languages, and stories — growing into one family. IVC is where deep, lasting friendships are built across every nationality and background.

03

Service

We serve because Christ served first. Through volunteer work and acts of love, we put our faith into action and make a concrete difference in the lives of those around us in Seoul.

What We Do

Life in IVC

Weekly Prayer

Every Friday, we gather in prayer and Scripture — the spiritual heart of everything IVC does.

Volunteer Service

Serving the vulnerable in Seoul — shelters, communities, making Christ's love visible.

Retreats

Days of retreat and recollection to encounter God and renew our calling to love and serve.

Faith Formation

Sessions to grow in knowledge of the Catholic faith — open to Catholics and non-Catholics alike.

International Exchange

Connecting Catholic youth across cultures and nations in the universal call to love.
