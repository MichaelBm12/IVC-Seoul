A Community Open to Everyone

IVC — the International Volunteers Community — is a youth Catholic community in Seoul, under the Archdiocese of Seoul. We are a family of 100+ young people from different cultures, backgrounds, and nationalities who come together to build friendships and serve in the name of Jesus Christ.

You Are Warmly Invited

Whether you've been Catholic all your life or you're simply curious — there is a place for you here. IVC is open to all youth who want to grow, serve, and belong.

🌟

You don't have to be Catholic to join. IVC welcomes everyone who wants to build friendships and serve in the name of Christ.

  • ✓ Based in or visiting Seoul
  • ✓ Open heart for community and service
