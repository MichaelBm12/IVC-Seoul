Duc in Altum — "Put out into the deep"

Who We Are

A youth Catholic family under the Archdiocese of Seoul. 100+ young people from all over the globe building friendships in Christ.

Open to All

You don't have to be Catholic to join. We welcome everyone who wants to grow, serve, and belong in Seoul.

What We Do

One Friday / Month

The Holy Rosary

A serene evening of meditative prayer and Marian devotion. We contemplate the mysteries of Christ through Mother Mary, offering intentions for the world and peace in Korea.

Every Saturday

Gospel Sharing

Our weekly tradition where we break open the Word. We reflect on the Sunday Gospel, sharing testimonies on living faith as young pilgrims in Seoul.

Twice a Month

Community Volunteering

Faith in action. We serve the marginalized and elderly, acting as the "hands and feet of Jesus" within our local neighborhood through diverse service projects.

Journey to 2027

WYD Seoul 2027

Preparing for World Youth Day! We participate in archdiocesan festivals, retreats, and leadership training to welcome the global Church to Korea.

First Thursday / Month

Eucharistic Adoration

A sacred hour of silence with the Blessed Sacrament. Often led by the Archbishop, this is our time to find peace in Christ and pray for our community.
