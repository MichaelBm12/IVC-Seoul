A Community Open to Everyone

IVC — the International Volunteers Community — is a youth Catholic community under the Archdiocese of Seoul. We are a family of 100+ young people from different cultures and nationalities building friendships in Christ.

You Are Warmly Invited

🌟 You don't have to be Catholic to join.

Whether you're Catholic or just curious, there is a place for you. We welcome all youth who want to grow, serve, and belong.

  • ✓ All nationalities and backgrounds welcome
  • ✓ Based in or visiting Seoul
  • ✓ Open heart for community and service
