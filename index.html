Duc in Altum — "Put out into the deep"

Who We Are

A youth Catholic family under the Archdiocese of Seoul. 100+ internationals building friendships in Christ.

Open to All

You don't have to be Catholic. We welcome all youth who want to grow, belong, and serve in Seoul.

What We Do

Growing together in faith, service, and community.

One Friday / Month

Holy Rosary

Meditative prayer gathering to contemplate the mysteries of Christ through Mother Mary, offering intentions for peace.

Every Saturday

Gospel Sharing

Weekly reflections on the Sunday Gospel, sharing faith insights as international pilgrims living in the heart of Seoul.

Twice a Month

Volunteering

Serving the marginalized as the hands and feet of Jesus, spreading love through local service projects.

Journey to 2027

WYD Events

Preparations, retreats, and festivals as we journey toward the World Youth Day Seoul 2027.

First Thursday

Eucharistic Adoration

Silent worship with the Blessed Sacrament, often joined by the Archbishop to seek spiritual depth.
