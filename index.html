Duc in Altum

IVC Seoul is a youth Catholic community under the Archdiocese of Seoul. We are a family of 100+ young people from different cultures, backgrounds, and nationalities who come together to build friendships and serve in the name of Jesus Christ.

Open to Everyone

Whether you've been Catholic all your life or you're simply curious — there is a place for you here. IVC welcomes everyone who wants to grow, serve, and belong.
