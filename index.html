Who We Are

A youth Catholic family under the Archdiocese of Seoul. 100+ internationals building friendships in Christ.

Open to All

You don't have to be Catholic. We welcome all youth who want to grow and serve in Seoul.

What We Do

One Friday / Month

Holy Rosary

Contemplating the mysteries of Christ through Mother Mary, offering intentions for peace in Korea.

Every Saturday

Gospel Sharing

Weekly reflections on the Sunday Word and our lives as international pilgrims in Seoul.

Twice a Month

Volunteering

Serving the marginalized as the hands and feet of Jesus within our local neighborhood.

Journey to 2027

WYD Events

Spiritual retreats and festivals as we prepare for World Youth Day Seoul 2027.

First Thursday

Eucharistic Adoration

Silent worship with the Blessed Sacrament, often joined by the Archbishop of Seoul.
